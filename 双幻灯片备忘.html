Xinhe Shi

I lead the research team on humanoid robot learning at the Embodied AI Center of Shanghai AI Lab. Our research aims to empower humanoid robots with the ability to acquire versatile skills and autonomously adapt to complex environments, leveraging prior knowledge demonstrated by humans in their daily lives. Previously, I conducted research in areas including character animation and simulation, efficient perception models—such as BiSeNet V1 and V2—and 3D reconstruction.
